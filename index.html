About AGJ Data Infotech

AGJ Data Infotech, founded in 2008 and headquartered in New Delhi, India, specializes in market research fieldwork services across India and neighboring countries like Nepal, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, and Bangladesh. We have established ourselves as a trusted name in the field, offering a variety of services to ensure client satisfaction and quality deliverables.

Over the years, AGJ Data Infotech has expanded its services beyond market research to include data entry, transcription, note-taking, and moderation services. We work closely with clients to provide tailored solutions that meet their unique business needs, no matter how complex the task may be.

Our vision is to be the most reliable customized market research partner in the region, delivering honest, dependable results through our skilled fieldwork teams. We aim to build long-lasting relationships with our clients and create a positive impact on their businesses. Our mission is to assist leading corporations in achieving customer satisfaction by providing effective, actionable insights through robust research methodologies.

With expertise in both qualitative and quantitative research, we empower businesses to understand consumer behavior, trends, and market dynamics. Whether through mystery shopping, online research, or central location tests, our goal is always to deliver accurate, timely data that drives informed decision-making for our clients.
